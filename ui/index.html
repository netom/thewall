Instant. Messaging.

You don't have to install, or download anything. The Wall works on your PC or mobile device instantly.

Security.

The Wall uses strong encryption to make truly secret communication possible.

Privacy.

The Wall uses a simple, proven trick to keep your conversations safe. Only those who know the URL can read and write to you Wall.

Simplicity.

Start a conversation without registering. Share the URL to invite others.

Keep it short.

A post must be at most 200 characters long, and a wall can only keep 100 posts. If a new post arrives, and your Wall is already 100 posts long, the oldest one is deleted to make room for it.

Off the record.

Walls are forgotten 24 hours past the last post. Nothing is saved on disks, everything is lost on power or software failures, attacks, confiscations.
